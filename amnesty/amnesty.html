Fear of the Sky is a Virtual Reality project for Amnesty International showcasing the realities of the Syrian Civil War. This is a dramatic trailer currently in progress cut together using footage from BBC and Amnesty International. Using cinematic narrative conventions to build a tense atmosphere I carefully crafted the trailer to show both the horrors of the Syrian War as well as the features of the V.R. experience. I also recored audio with a Yeti mic and a pop filter.

Project Name : Amnesty International Fear of the Sky

Type : Narrative Film-making, Motion Graphic, Video Trailer

Tools Used : Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator, Audition
