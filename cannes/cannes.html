Using footage from Sicario, an offcial selection from the Festival De Cannes, I crafted an interactive image as a piece of the brand identity. Matching the typography's appearance to the sound of japanese drums each poster has the potential to be a wholly unique composition. The identity extends to movie passes and a poster that requires physical manipulation as well. The identity utilizes sight, sound, and touch to provide an immersive experience.

Project Name : Festival De Cannes

Type : Message Campaign, Communication Design, Brand Identity

Tools Used : Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator
