The Art Annex is a venue on campus comprising of a joint effort between the Art Department and UC Davis's Art Administration with the goal of providing a space for performance and expression. I came up with "Art With Us" as campaign to invite UC Davis students to join in the satisfaction of expressing themselves in whatever medium possible. The project spans graphic, interactive advertising, and web. Check out the UI and Web Design Invision prototypes.

Project Name : Art With Us

Type : Campaign Design, UI + UX Development, Interactive Design

Tools Used : Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, InvisionApp, Lasercutter
