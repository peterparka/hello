Columbia University's Center for the Grief is a research center that focuses on helping people with Complicated Grief with their condition. The center approached Adobe with the pro bono prospect of helping redesign the website and user experience to help better communicate their brand message. My role on the team was to help work out the user experience for the therapist location, onboarding sign ups, and menu transitions. Each presented their own challenges with interaction design being key to help the Center communicate their message through digital mediums. I focused on making the traversal of information on the site and frictionless as possible. I also helped to research opportunities to convert users to members of the site.

Project Name : The Center for Complicated Grief

Type : User Experience, User Interface, Interaction Design

Tools Used : Adobe XD, Sketch, Principle
