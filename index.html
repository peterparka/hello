Hello, I'm a San Francisco based Experience Designer working with Adobe's Design Lab. From visual to product to UX and even exhibition design, I love finding narrative in the process.

Having a vision is important, but executing that vision is paramount. And that process comes from great research, collaboration, and an intrepid attitude.

I've worked with designers from Adobe, AKQA, Razorfish, Adaptive Path, Argonaut, and now you!
